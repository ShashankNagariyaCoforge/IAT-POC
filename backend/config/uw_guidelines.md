# IAT Insurance — Underwriting Guidelines for Management Liability

## General Underwriting Philosophy
IAT Insurance Group underwrites Management Liability lines with a focus on risk quality, governance maturity,
and financial stability. Submissions are evaluated on a holistic basis, considering both quantitative and
qualitative factors.

## Appetite & Target Market
- **Preferred**: Private and non-profit entities with revenues under $500M
- **Acceptable**: Public companies with strong governance structures
- **Avoid**: Companies in active litigation, financial distress, or recent restatements

## Coverage Lines
- Directors & Officers (D&O)
- Employment Practices Liability (EPL)
- Fiduciary Liability
- Crime / Fidelity
- Cyber Liability (blended with management liability package)

## Key Underwriting Factors

### Entity Risk Factors
1. **Years in Business**: Established businesses (5+ years) preferred. Startups require enhanced scrutiny.
2. **Entity Structure**: Public companies have higher D&O exposure due to SEC requirements.
3. **Industry Sector**: NAICS code analysis required for sector-specific exposures.
4. **Revenue / Employee Count**: Larger organizations carry higher EPL exposure.

### Governance & Controls
- Board composition and independence
- Audit committee presence and activity
- Internal controls and compliance programs
- Prior SEC filings / restatements (for public entities)

### Financial Health Indicators
- Profitability trends (3-year lookback preferred)
- Debt-to-equity ratio
- Cash flow stability
- Recent M&A activity (increases D&O exposure significantly)

### Claims & Loss History
- Prior D&O / EPL / Fiduciary claims in last 5 years
- Pending litigation or regulatory investigations
- EEOC charges or wage-and-hour class actions
- Any restatements of financial statements

## Pricing Considerations
- Base rate driven by revenue band and coverage selected
- Experience mod applied based on loss history
- Surcharges for: high employee count (>500), financial distress, public entity
- Credits for: long-term relationship, risk management programs, clean loss history

## Referral Triggers (Requires Senior UW Review)
- Any prior claim in the last 3 years
- Revenue exceeding $250M
- Public company with market cap < $100M
- Merger or acquisition in last 12 months
- More than 20% revenue from a single customer
- Financial losses in 2 of last 3 years

## Auto-Decline Criteria
- Active SEC investigation or formal enforcement action
- Insolvency proceeding or Chapter 11 within last 5 years
- Prior felony conviction of any executive officer
- Terrorism / sanctions exposure (OFAC screening required)

## Subjectivities — Common Requirements
- Most recent 3 years audited financials
- Completed ACORD application or supplemental questionnaire
- Loss runs for prior 5 years (if claims exist)
- Copy of prior policy declarations page
- For public entities: most recent proxy statement and 10-K

## Territory Notes
- Primary Rating State drives primary jurisdiction exposure
- Multi-state operations require aggregate state exposure assessment
- International operations: confirm jurisdiction of coverage
